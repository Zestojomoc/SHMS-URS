About the Structural Health Monitoring System

Shared public information for SHM, NFD, and Drift Monitoring

Project Overview

The Structural Health Monitoring System is a centralized web-based platform designed to monitor structural vibration behavior, identify natural frequency and damping characteristics, and evaluate inter-story drift performance of the structure using Raspberry Shake 4D data and engineering-based analysis.

Structural Health Monitoring

Monitors vibration response and structural condition to assess overall system behavior and detect unusual activity.

Natural Frequency & Damping

Identifies dynamic properties of the structure through frequency-domain analysis and damping estimation.

Inter-Story Drift Monitoring

Evaluates floor-level displacement behavior and drift-related structural performance indicators.
