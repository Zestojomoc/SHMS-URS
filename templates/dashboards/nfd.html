Project Overview • Sensor Specs • Research Team

Project Overview

This Structural Health Monitoring System (SHMS) is designed to monitor vibration response, identify dynamic properties (natural frequency and damping), and support structural condition assessment for the Science & Technology Building through continuous data collection and analysis.

Sensor Specifications
